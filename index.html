Mental Wellness vs. Mental Health



Mental Wellness

Mental wellness is being in a good state of mind, feeling comfortable or content. You are able to make choices that don't have negative reprocussions to your social or physical well-being.

Mental Health

Mental health is a general term for the way a person is functioning emotionally, socially and physchologically. It does not refer to a single state of someone's mental well-being, whether negative or positive.
